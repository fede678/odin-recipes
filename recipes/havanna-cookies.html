Havanna style lemon cookies

Description

This is a recipe for Havannah style lemon cookies. Credits to @Silnari on X.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 160g Butter
  • 112g Powdered sugar
  • Zest from 1 lemon
  • 1 Egg
  • 250g All purpose flour

Lemon filling

  • 30g Butter
  • 150g Powdered sugar
  • Zest from 1 lemon
  • 15ml Lemon juice

Extras

  • Chocolate coating

Steps

Dough

  1. Make a shortcrust by combining all the ingredients for the dough together in a mixer until you get a smooth and uniform dough.
  2. Leave to rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Stretch and cut into squares.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 10 minutes.

Filling

  1. Whisk the butter with 2/3 of the sugar until you get an uniform paste.
  2. Add the rest of the sugar, the lemon zest, and the lemon juice.
  3. Whisk until all ingredients are well combined.

Final steps

  1. Assemble the cookies by making a "sandwich" with 2 cookies and some filling. Let them cool.
  2. Once cold, coat them with the chocolate.
  3. Let cool again until the coating hardens and enjoy!
